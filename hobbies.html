My Hobbies

Particularly, my dream is to become an airline pilot and that is how I want to live my life. However, flight training can be expensive. To pay for flight training, I would need to work and make money. This leads me into my next hobby.

Web development

I like creating websites, and I figured that it would be a great way to finance my dream career of becoming an airline pilot. This would help me pay for flight training, and I also get to build websites for other people in return. Currently, I am learning how to develop websites, which would be a valuable skill for my future.
